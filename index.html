About Me.

I grew up behind a shop counter in Leicester, where I learned resilience, hard work, and the value of community. Today, I’m an Economics undergraduate at the University of Leicester, passionate about finance, investment banking, and politics.

I’ve earned Bloomberg certifications, completed the AmplifyMe Finance Simulation, and been selected for the PIMCO Spring Insight Programme. As part of the Patchwork Foundation Masterclass 2025, I contributed to a £100bn+ mock government spending review and was invited to No.10 Downing Street.

Beyond my studies, I’ve worked as a Lead Tutor at Kumon and Assistant Manager at Essential Savers for nearly six years. I’m also proud to have received the King’s Award for Voluntary Service and the Lord-Lieutenant’s Award for Young People. My goal is simple: to represent, uplift, and lead with purpose.
